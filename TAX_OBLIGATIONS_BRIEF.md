# Philippine Tax Obligations Brief

> Extracted from The Present Value — BIR Compliance Calendar & Calculator  
> Jurisdiction: Philippines | Authority: Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR)  
> Governing law: NIRC as amended by TRAIN Law (RA 10963), EOPT (RA 11976), and related revenue regulations.

---

## 1. Tax Regimes (Self-Employed / Mixed Income)

| Regime | Rate | Who qualifies |
|---|---|---|
| 8% Flat Income Tax | 8% of gross sales/receipts | Gross sales ≤ ₱3M; in lieu of income tax + percentage tax |
| Graduated Income Tax | 0–35% per bracket | Default; applies to all income levels |
| Optional Standard Deduction (OSD) | 40% of gross income deducted | Elected in lieu of itemized deductions |
| Itemized Deductions | Actual allowable expenses | Must be substantiated with receipts/records |

### Graduated Income Tax Brackets (TRAIN Law)

| Annual Taxable Income | Rate |
|---|---|
| Up to ₱250,000 | 0% |
| ₱250,001 – ₱400,000 | 15% on excess over ₱250,000 |
| ₱400,001 – ₱800,000 | ₱22,500 + 20% on excess over ₱400,000 |
| ₱800,001 – ₱2,000,000 | ₱102,500 + 25% on excess over ₱800,000 |
| ₱2,000,001 – ₱8,000,000 | ₱402,500 + 30% on excess over ₱2,000,000 |
| Over ₱8,000,000 | ₱2,202,500 + 35% on excess over ₱8,000,000 |

## 2. Corporate Income Tax

| Tax | Rate | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Regular Corporate Income Tax (RCIT) | 25% (20% for small corps) | 20% applies to domestic corps with net taxable income ≤ ₱5M and total assets ≤ ₱100M |
| Minimum Corporate Income Tax (MCIT) | 2% of gross income | Applies from the 4th year of operations; whichever is higher vs. RCIT |

---

## 3. Business Taxes

| Tax | Rate | Threshold / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Value Added Tax (VAT) | 12% on sales/services | Mandatory registration once gross sales exceed ₱3M/year |
| Percentage Tax | 3% of gross sales/receipts | For non-VAT-registered taxpayers not using the 8% flat rate |
| Government VAT Withholding | 5% of gross payment withheld by gov't agency | Claimable as input VAT credit via Form 2307 against 2550Q |

---

## 4. Withholding Taxes

| Tax | Rate | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Expanded Withholding Tax (EWT) | 5–10% (varies by transaction) | Withheld by payor; creditable against payee's income tax |
| Final Withholding Tax (FWT) | Varies | On dividends, royalties, interest; not creditable |
| Creditable Withholding Tax on Compensation | Per graduated bracket | Withheld from employee salaries monthly |

---

## 5. Other Taxes

| Tax | Notes |
|---|---|
| Documentary Stamp Tax (DST) | On taxable documents: leases, loans, debt instruments, certain stock transactions |
| BMBE Exemption | Barangay Micro Business Enterprises (RA 9178) are exempt from income tax |

## 9. Key Thresholds & Rules

- **VAT registration threshold:** ₱3,000,000 gross annual sales/receipts
- **8% flat rate eligibility:** Gross sales must not exceed ₱3,000,000
- **MCIT applies:** From the 4th year of corporate operations onward; 2% of gross income vs. RCIT, whichever is higher
- **Monthly 2550M abolished:** Under EOPT (RA 11976); VAT is now filed quarterly only via 2550Q
- **EWT sworn declaration:** Filed by January 15 each year by self-employed/mixed income earners qualifying for a lower withholding rate
- **13th month pay:** Must be released by December 24; tax-exempt up to ₱90,000
- **Rent control (RA 9653):** Affects deductibility rules for residential rental income
- **E-invoicing (RR 11-2025):** Structured electronic invoices required by December 31, 2026
